1 Elementary School

The school uses the International Primary Curriculum (IPC) framework to deliver the National Curriculum of Nepal in a thematic and progressive approach. The IPC is an internationally recognized curriculum framework which is flexible and accommodates national curricula of any country.

2 Secondary School

Students are required to appear for the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) in grade 10 conducted by the National Examination Board, Nepal. In grade 9 and 10, students will be offered subjects as per the national curriculum along with two optional subjects of their choice

3 Cambridge International 'A' Level

The internationally acclaimed and prestigious Cambridge International A Level with Global Perspectives will also be offered at KWS.This two-year course is equivalent to grade 11 and 12 and is admired by Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE).

4 Pre-Medical & Engineering Preparations

The trend shows that a majority of students pursuing Science stream hope to get entry into an undergraduate course in Medical Science or Engineering for which Plus 2 graduates must qualify in the nation–wide competitive entrance Engineering And Medical.

5 Guidance

At KWS, we provide guidance, counselling and learning support to the students who lag behind with their learning and adaptation.Our experienced guidance counsellor will help students to cope with the difficulties they are facing for their benefits.
